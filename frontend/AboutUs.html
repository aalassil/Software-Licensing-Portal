OUR MISSION

Making Software Licensing Safer for Everyone

We make the use and purchase of software licenses easier between users. Our goal is to have an interactive platform and eliminate any complexities associated with software license management for both clients and providers.

We offer various features and benefits for both clients and providers.

For providers:

  • Generating and associating serial numbers
  • Enabling and disabling serial numbers
  • Managing clients' accounts

For clients:

  • Acquiring new and renewing existing licenses
  • Managing serial numbers
  • Association of purchased licenses
